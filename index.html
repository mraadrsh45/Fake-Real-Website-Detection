Cyber Security Tips

🔒
Verify Website Security

Always check for HTTPS and valid SSL certificates before entering sensitive information.

🔍
Inspect URLs Carefully

Look for misspellings or unusual domain names that might indicate phishing attempts.

🛡️
Use Strong Passwords

Create unique, complex passwords and enable two-factor authentication where possible.

📱
Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your operating system and applications to patch security vulnerabilities.

📧
Be Email Smart

Never click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown senders.

Common Cyber Threats

🎣
Phishing Attacks

Fraudulent attempts to obtain sensitive information through deceptive emails or websites.

💉
Malware

Malicious software designed to damage or gain unauthorized access to systems.

🔐
Ransomware

Malware that encrypts files and demands payment for their release.

👥
Social Engineering

Psychological manipulation to trick people into revealing confidential information.
